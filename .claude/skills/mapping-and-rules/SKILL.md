# Mapping And Rules

- Convert source and target understanding into explicit mapping logic.
- Do not claim mappings are complete if the source sample, target contract, or business rules are partial.
- Make joins, derivations, defaults, validations, and exceptions concrete.
- Update `transformation-understanding.md` and `data-dependency-map.md` when the mapping changes shared understanding.
- Prefer implementation-grade rules over vague summaries.
