# Delivery Readiness

- Turn analysed capabilities into a practical delivery sequence with owners, gates, and deliverables.
- Separate deliverables that can be generated now from deliverables blocked by missing evidence or approvals.
- Make operational expectations, rollback posture, and support ownership visible.
- Keep `delivery-implementation-plan.md` concrete and execution-ready.
- Distinguish confirmed readiness from open decisions or blocked approvals.
