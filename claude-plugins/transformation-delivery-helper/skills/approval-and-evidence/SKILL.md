# Approval And Evidence

- Identify who needs to approve what and why.
- Separate evidence that is actually present from evidence that still needs to be collected.
- State the evidence pack, validation output, or risk summary required for each decision.
- Keep delivery planning aligned with governance and auditability expectations.
- Escalate missing approval inputs clearly instead of implying the work is already ready.
